The Best Movies According to Faiyaz

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter Series

Harry Potter is my all-time favourite Hollywood movie. When I was in class 8, I used to read the books first and then watch the movies. It would be so fascinating to watch the characters act the way the author i.e. J.K. Rowling wanted them to. Today when I watch them, it brings back the nostalgia of my school days.

Fantastic Beasts

Fantastic Beasts is a 3 part series which is a prequel to the Harry Potter franchise. This series tells us about the wizarding world before the era of Harry Potter. It is a must waqtch for Harry Potter fans.

Bajrangi Bhaijaan

This is a Bollywood movie played by Salman Khan. The message that the movie gives is truly inspiring. It tells us how a man by the name of Pawan who is a hindu devotee goes out of his way to reunite a muslim girl with her mother who lost her during her visit to India.
